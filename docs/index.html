Emits a ~18 kHz tone (near the top of adult hearing) and measures the Doppler shift of its reflection off a moving object. Works best at close range on larger objects — you walking, a passing car, a thrown ball. Small fast objects like hockey pucks are inconsistent.

iPhone note: Safari routes speaker output to the quiet earpiece when the mic is active. If Both mode stays at —, open the page on a laptop in Emit only and on the iPhone in Listen only. The math is identical either way.
